About Us

R Square Designs is an Architectural Design Studio and Project Management Team started in July 2008 which provides all-round services for both residential, corporate & commercial interior fit-out projects.

Based in Mumbai, INDIA, R Square Designs specializes in exquisite luxurious designs for both residential & commercial premises and offer complete professional services in all related fields. If you require a professional and pro-active team to manage or remodel your existing premises, relocate or open new premises, R Square Designs can satisfy and exceed all your requirements. Whilst we are a ONE STOP SHOP for all your needs, we recognize that all Clients are different, and this reflects in the type, style and amount of services each requires.

Our products which are exceptionally exclusive and custom made as per the taste of individual clients and are of the highest standards. We believe in providing our clients with residential apartments with every detail as a personal touch for luxury and perfection.

A fast-growing reputation for high-quality interior finishes with strict financial control and timely delivery, R Square Designs has moulded itself into a company which can plan and execute interior fit-out projects with utmost efficiency and maintaining superior finishes with the balance of timelines and budgets.

In 2008 R Square Designs started working with some major MNC banks on interior fit-out projects. We have successfully worked with DSP Merrill Lynch (Bank Of America), Macquarie Securities (India) Pvt. Ltd., Bain Capital Advisors (India) Pvt. Ltd., D S Purbhoodas, Ambit Capital (India) Pvt. Ltd., Ashmore Investment Advisors (India) Pvt. Ltd., Superfund (India) Pvt. Ltd. Daiwa Capital Markets (India) Pvt. Ltd. Ashmore Securities, Kyowa Hakko Bio (India) Pvt. Ltd. Asahi Kasei (India) Pvt. Ltd. and many such reputed Multinational Companies throughout India. Additionally we have also completed the interior fit-out for The Spanish Consulate in Mumbai, India along with other numerous high-end interior fit-outs for residential premises throughout India.

Having worked with such big names in the Investment Banking sector we have been able to develop a keen sense of knowledge for timely completion of project within the agreed budget and keeping the interior finishes at all stages and the final output to be of the highest market standards.

We have also done many installations for a UK based dealing desk manufacturing company SBFI London (UK). We have done SBFI Dealing Desk installations for SBFI directly to our clients at UBS Securities (India) Pvt. Ltd., Goldman Sachs (India) Pvt.Ltd., CLSA (India).

R Square Designs has been formed as a wholly constituted Indian Company. Thus placing the Company in the perfect position to expand its market share and enhance its reputation for meeting or exceeding our Clients requirements through a process of continuous improvement, strict control, timely completion and offering the very best value for money on behalf of our Clients.

Architectural Designing

MEP Service Layouts

Detailed MEP Service designs and layouts including Electrics, Fire Safety, CCTV, Access Control, Security Monitoring, Plumbing, Data Cabling, VESDA, Water Leak Detection, etc., etc.

HVAC Designing

HVAC Designing for both low side including window units, split units and cassette units and on high side ductable units and precision aircon units i.e. CRAC units for server rooms and/or data centres

Data Centre and Server Room Setups
